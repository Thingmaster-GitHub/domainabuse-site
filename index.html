How Does Communication Occur on the Internet?

Our computers can do so many things for us from a click in just milliseconds. However, what happens in these milliseconds are a lot more complex than we think.

Here's what happens when you search up a website URL on the internet:

Our computer sends a request to the HTTP to get the requested URL. HTTP is a protocol (standardized rules for communication systems) that is designed to transfer information between our network devices. Our computers prioritize protocols of speed and accuracy when transmitting information. Speed is called UDP (User Datagram Protocol) and accuracy is called TCP (Transmission Control Protocol).
